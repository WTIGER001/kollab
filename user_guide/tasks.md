# User Guide: My Tasks Dashboard

The **My Tasks** dashboard is a centralized space where you can review, track, and navigate all checklist action items assigned to you across all documents in your workspace.

---

## 👤 Accessing My Tasks
1. Click on your **User Profile** dropdown in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
2. Select **My Tasks** from the menu.
3. The editor view will switch to show your tasks dashboard.

---

## 📋 How Task Assignments Work
When editing any document in the workspace:
- Creating a checklist item and typing **`@username`** (e.g. `@jbauer`) assigns that specific task item to the user.
- Typing **`//`** allows you to assign a due date to that checklist item.
- When the document is saved, the system automatically parses these tags and updates the dashboard index.

---

## 🎨 Dashboard Features

### 1. Document Grouping
Tasks are grouped by the **document** they reside on, allowing you to understand the project context of each task.

### 2. Color-Coded Due Date Indicators
The dashboard automatically calculates deadline urgency and styles due dates with visual badges:
- 🔴 **Red (Overdue)**: The due date has passed.
- 🟠 **Orange (Due Today)**: The task is due by the end of today.
- 🟢 **Green / Muted (Future)**: The task is due in the future.

### 3. Click-to-Navigate Redirects
Task IDs and checklist checkboxes are synced via document saves. To view or check off a task:
- Click anywhere on the task row in the dashboard.
- Arkollab will instantly load the correct workspace space, open the target document, and scroll directly to the task list so you can view it in context and mark it complete.
